A Reminder





I wait no more, so I challenge myself.

I WILL commit myself fully to whatever I intend,
because change can begin only through action.
I WILL seize the day and perfect the present,
because success is not a goal, but a process.
I WILL dive into essence beneath every task,
because the right way shall fly me to the stars.

The timing could not have been better.
The opportunties are vivid as they are myriad.
The question is, how soon WILL I be ready?
